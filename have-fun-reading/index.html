Be less good*, have more fun

Supporting young readers: a workshop for grown-ups

Wednesday 15th July · 6pm–7pm

Southville Centre, Beauley Road

£10 / pay what you can

Story Club was inspired by the power of having fun in creating confident readers and writers. We know parents and carers understand that enjoying reading is a good thing, but we also know that life can quickly turn it into a chore. This workshop is an introduction to some tools for keeping things fun while enjoying stories.

What to expect from the workshop

  • A friendly literacy expert, Lily Eastwood, a trained teacher with over 10 years' experience designing and delivering evidence based reading and writing programmes focused on enjoyment.
  • Peer support as you hear from other parents and carers in the room, and share your own experiences if you would like.
  • Time to reflect on what "good reading" is and where our own relationship with reading comes from.
  • Bite-sized chunks of research that underpins the practices of promoting reading enjoyment.
  • Practical ideas for reading at home.
  • Biscuits!
